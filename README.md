Barely-Visible Kingdoms: Exploring Mold, Light Spectroscopy, Resonance, and Acetic Concentration with Dr. Melody Bouck and Dr. Morgan Gleaves

Explore the groundbreaking research of Dr. Melody Bouck and Dr. Morgan Gleaves on mold, using light spectroscopy, resonance, and acetic concentration. Discover how these insights impact occupational health, pathogen detection, and the future of microbial forensics. Written by Dr. Correo Hofstad.
